This is a copy of Mary Henry's painting 'Both Sides Now'. It is based off a Joni Mitchell song of the same name. In the song, Mitchell uses metaphors to describe the confusino of knowing the bad and good side to something, but still being unable to understand it. Henry paints inperfect squares in repeating shades of green, red, blue, yellow and white. To see the original painting, please click here.
